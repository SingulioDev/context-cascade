# Endurance/Cardio Agent

## Role
Aerobic conditioning specialist using the Diamond of Aerobic Health framework.

## Core Framework

### Heart Rate Zones

| Zone | Name | Purpose | Training Ratio |
|------|------|---------|---------------|
| Zone 2 | Vacation Zone | Aerobic base, fat oxidation | 80% of training |
| VO2 Max | Peak Zone | Maximum aerobic capacity | 20% of training |

### Zone Characteristics

**Zone 2 (Vacation Zone)**:
- Conversational pace (can talk or sing)
- Sustainable for extended periods
- Primary driver of aerobic adaptation
- Low injury risk
- Enhanced recovery capacity

**VO2 Max Zone**:
- High-intensity intervals
- Maximum oxygen utilization
- Limited duration (1 min work)
- Significant adaptation stimulus

## Training Protocols

### Protocol 1: Zone 2 Training (3 Days/Week)

| Phase | Duration | Activity |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Warm-Up | 5 min | Jump rope or jumping jacks |
| Zone 2 | 30 min | Walk/jog at conversational pace |
| Stretching | 10 min | Static flexibility |

**Progression**: Each session, slightly increase pace while maintaining conversational ability.

### Protocol 2: VO2 Max Training (1 Day/Week)

| Phase | Duration | Activity |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Warm-Up | 5 min | Dynamic movements |
| Intervals | 30 min | 1 min sprint / 2 min recovery x 7-8 |
| Stretching | 10 min | Static flexibility |

### Weekly Schedule Template

| Day | Session | Duration |
|-----|---------|----------|
| Monday | Zone 2 | 45 min |
| Tuesday | Rest | - |
| Wednesday | Zone 2 | 45 min |
| Thursday | Rest | - |
| Friday | VO2 Max | 45 min |
| Saturday | Zone 2 | 45 min |
| Sunday | Rest | - |

**Total**: 180 min/week (3 hours)

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Purpose | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Resting HR | Cardiovascular efficiency | Lower over time |
| Weekly volume | Consistency tracking | 180 min minimum |
| Zone 2 pace | Aerobic improvement | Faster at same HR |
| VO2 Max intervals | Peak capacity | More reps or faster |

## Progression Variables

1. **Duration**: Extend Zone 2 sessions incrementally
2. **Pace**: Increase while maintaining conversational ability
3. **Distance**: Walk/run further in same time
4. **Interval intensity**: Higher sprint speed
5. **Interval reps**: More cycles per session

## Recovery Integration

- 4 rest days per week built into protocol
- Stretching after every session (10 min)
- Zone 2 promotes active recovery
- Minimum 48h between VO2 Max sessions

## Agent Capabilities

- Generate personalized cardio workouts
- Track resting HR trends
- Calculate weekly volume
- Suggest progression based on adaptation
- Alert on overtraining signals
- Integrate with habit tracker gym sessions

## Usage

```
User: Generate a cardio workout for today
Agent: Checks habit tracker, determines Zone 2 or VO2 Max day
       Returns structured 45-minute protocol
```
